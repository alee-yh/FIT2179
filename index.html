Does Money Buy Happiness in Malaysia?

Exploring how income, geography and
identity shape happiness across Malaysia.

Malaysians today are earning more than before.
Yet happiness is not distributed equally across
the nation. Some states report strong well-being
despite lower incomes, while wealthier regions do
not always top the happiness rankings. This
visual story explores whether economic prosperity
truly leads to a happier life.

PART I

How Happy Are Malaysians?

Happiness in Malaysia extends beyond income alone.
The Malaysian Happiness Index (MHI) measures well-being
across multiple life domains, revealing how satisfaction
varies across states and communities.

What Shapes Happiness in Malaysia?

Malaysians report different levels of happiness across 13
life domains. Some aspects of life score consistently high,
while others reveal areas of dissatisfaction.

Not Every State Experiences Happiness the Same Way

Looking deeper into each life domain reveals
strong regional differences.States that perform
well in one domain may struggle in another.

Where are Malaysia's Happiest States?

Happiness is unevenly distributed across the country.
Geographic patterns reveal how social, economic, and
cultural factors influence well-being.

PART II

Does More Money Make Malaysians Happier?

Household income strongly shapes quality of life.
However, higher earnings do not always translate
into higher happiness levels.

Income and Happiness Don't Always Move Together

Wealthier states generally report higher happiness, but
the relationship is far from perfect. Some lower-income
states achieve suprisingly high well-being scores.

How Satisfied Are Malaysians With Their Income?

Financial satisfaction contributes to happiness, but it
is only part of a large picture involving social
relationships, health, and daily well-being.

How is Income Distributed Across Each State?

Median income alone cannot fully capture inequality.
Looking across percentiles reveals how income is
distributed within each state.

PART III

Happiness is About More Than Money

Identity, lifestyle, and personal priorities all influence how Malaysians experience happiness.

Do Men and Women Experience Happiness Differently?

Gender differences in happiness vary across states.
Lines connecting the two scores show direction and
size of gender gap.

Different Communities Value Different Aspects of Life

Happiness is shaped differently across ethnic groups.

How Does Happiness Change Across Age?

Happiness evolves throughout life and across different priorities.

So, Can Money Buy Happiness?

Income clearly improves comfort and opportunity. Yet across Malaysia, happiness is also shaped by community, identity, relationships, health and family. Wealth matters, but it is not the whole story.
